The Night Watch

Artist: Rembrandt van Rijn

4.8/5

"The Night Watch" is a famous painting by Rembrandt, completed in 1642. It is known for its dramatic use of light and shadow and its depiction of a city militia preparing for action. This masterpiece remains one of the most celebrated works of the Dutch Golden Age.

Year: 1642

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 363 cm × 437 cm (142.9 in × 172.0 in)

Location: Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam

Price: 25 Million
