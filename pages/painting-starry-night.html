Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

4.7/5

"Starry Night" is one of the most iconic paintings by Vincent van Gogh, created in 1889. This masterpiece depicts a night sky filled with swirling clouds, shining stars, and a bright crescent moon. The village below is peaceful, with a church steeple reaching toward the sky. Van Gogh's use of bold colors and dynamic brushstrokes captures the emotional intensity of the scene.


Year: 1889

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 73.7 cm × 92.1 cm (29 in × 36.25 in)

Location: Museum of Modern Art, New York

Price: 82.5 Million
