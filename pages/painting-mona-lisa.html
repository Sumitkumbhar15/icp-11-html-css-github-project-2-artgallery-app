Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

4.7/5

"Mona Lisa" is one of the most famous portraits in the world, created by Leonardo da Vinci between 1503 and 1506. The painting is known for the subject's enigmatic smile, detailed sfumato technique, and realistic portrayal. It is considered a masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance.


Year: 1503–1506

Medium: Oil on Poplar Panel

Dimensions: 77 cm × 53 cm (30 in × 21 in)

Location: Louvre Museum, Paris

Price: 30 Million
